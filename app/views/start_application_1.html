You are eligible to make an online claim for an illness, injury or condition you received in service

Please allow 1 hour to complete your online claim

To apply, you'll need:

  • contact details (phone number and address)
  • your National Insurance number
  • your service details
  • reference numbers to any previous claims made to Veterans UK for a service-related injury or illness

You may also need:

  • any incident report forms
  • any police reference numbers
  • any documents related to your condition, if you have them (diagnosis letters or hospital correspondence)
  • hospital details such as addresses, treatment dates and hospital record numbers
  • details of any treatments for your condition and the names of the healthcare professionals that have treated you

At the end of the form you will have the opportunity to upload supporting documents or send them to us in the post.

If you're unable to upload the requested documents during the application, you can still submit the application without them.

Or save your progress and return later

You can leave this application at any point and your progress will be saved. You can return to this application at any time until 26 May 2020

Need help filling out your application?

For questions about your application please contact Veterans UK:

If you need support from a welfare officer to complete this application then please contact Veterans Welfare service.
