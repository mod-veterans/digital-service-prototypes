Apply for compensation for a service-related injury or illness

You can use this service to apply for compensation for an injury, illness or condition you may have received during your service in the Armed Forces.

This service involves two schemes.

The Armed Forces Compensation Scheme applies to:

  • injury or illness is due to service on or after 6th April 2005

The War Pension Scheme applies to:

  • injury or illness is due to service on or before 6th April 2005

What you'll need

In order to use our online service Veterans UK will first need to verify your identity.

1 To apply for compensation online you will need:

  • your date of birth
  • your service number
  • your email address
2 Veterans UK will use this information to verify your identify within 24 hours.
3 Once verified, Veterans UK will email you a link to the online service or provide you with next steps.
